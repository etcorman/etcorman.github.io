About

Since December 2019 I am a CNRS researcher at LORIA in Nancy. I was a Post-Doctoral researcher at University of Toronto with Alec Jacobson and previously at Carnegie Mellon University in the Computer Science Department with Keenan Crane. I obtainded a PhD from the Ecole Polyetchnique under the supervision of Antonin Chambolle and Maks Ovsjanikov.

Research interests

  • Shape analysis
  • Non-rigid shape matching
  • Geometry Processing
